Lido - Staking Liquefied

Lido is a liquid staking protocol. One which is very flexible in terms of staking assets into proof of stake networks or protocols. Generally proof-of-stake requires assets to be locked in the stake. This makes these assets illiquid. Lido solves this problem. Lido lets users stake their assets for daily staking rewards. User can stake any amount of tokens - no minimum. When staking Lido you mint staked tokens which are pegged 1:1 to your initial stake. Your staked tokens can be used across the DeFi ecosystem to compound your yield. Lido lets you use your staked assets to gain yield on top of yield. Use your tokens (which earn daily staking rewards) as collateral, for lending, yield farming and more.

Lido achieves this by incentivizing liquidity providers on the secondary market, to provided liquidity to swap between the tokenized stake and the underlying asset. Curve is the go to for ETH swap for Lido. Lido's incentives keep a massive almost $5B TVL in the pool to exchange your tokenized stake into the underlying asset or vice versa. This allows Lido to keep its stake Liquid and thus allowing any user to exit the stake anytime.

Lido also distributes staking rewards from the Beacon chain, to all wallets holding stETH. This is generally around 5%. A liquid staking option allows a user the flexibiity to take part in staking while also keeping the said asset liquid. Now the user can use his asset to other activities. Some common examples are farming.

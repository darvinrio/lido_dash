Using yearn's steCRV vault

The Strategy
  • Deposit your stETH into Curve's steCRV pool
  • Deposit the steCRV LP token you have recieved in return into the Yearn's steCRV vault.
  • Sit back and relax

The Components
Curve's stETH/ETH pool is Lido's main route for establishing liquid staking. By allocating a pool to swap stETH to ETH, it essentially unlocks the staked ETH for a user.LPs to this pool are incentivized heavily by Lido and its one of the biggest Pools in the curve protocol. Yearn's steCRV vault which farms using curve's stETH/ETH liquidity pool is one of the most famous farming options available on the mainnet. It mainly uses two strategies. However recently, only one has be predominantly used.
It stakes the stETH/ETH LP - steCRV token into convex.finance, where it earns rewards in the form of CRV and CVX tokens. These tokens are harvested, swapped and deposited back in the form of steCRV tokens into the vault.

The risks
In terms of risk, this is a slightly more riskier option. This is because, this strategies uses three protocols, namely Yearn, Curve and Convex atop of Lido , to farm. This means, we are exposed to any exploit across any of these contracts. However, Yearn and Curve are battle tested protocols and considered as the foundation protocols for Ethereum DeFi. Though the same cannot be said of Convex, its still an audited protocol.
